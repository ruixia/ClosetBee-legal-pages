Data Deletion Instructions

If you signed up for ClosetBee and want to delete your account and associated data:

  1. Open the ClosetBee app.
  2. Go to Account.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your deletion request.

All user data including login credentials and uploaded images will be permanently deleted from our servers within 24 hours and backups within 30 days.

If you are unable to access the app, you may request deletion by emailing us at mike@closetbee.app.
